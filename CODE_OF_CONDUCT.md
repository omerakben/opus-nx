# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and constructive community for all contributors.

## Expected Behavior

1. Be respectful in language and feedback.
2. Focus critique on ideas and implementation, not people.
3. Share context and evidence when challenging technical decisions.
4. Help keep discussions actionable and specific.

## Unacceptable Behavior

1. Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
2. Repeated hostile or disruptive behavior.
3. Publishing private information without consent.
4. Bad-faith participation that blocks collaboration.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate for violations, including:

1. Warning
2. Temporary participation limits
3. Removal from project spaces

## Reporting

Report concerns through repository issues or direct maintainer contact channels used by the project.

When reporting, include:

1. What happened
2. Where it happened
3. When it happened
4. Any relevant context or evidence

All reports will be reviewed in good faith and handled with discretion.
